# Catalyst Grit v1.9.0

**Monitoring, Trends, and Resilience Signals**

This release adds immutable longitudinal snapshots, traceable trend series, personal and project dashboards, privacy-thresholded team/system conditions, recovery timelines, workflow outcome measures, repeated-friction summaries, system-change outcomes, sparse-data confidence states, and append-only human interpretation reviews.

No signal may be used to rank people, diagnose individuals, make hidden predictions, or automate employment or eligibility decisions.
