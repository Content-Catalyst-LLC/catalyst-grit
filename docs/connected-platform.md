# Connected Human-Systems Resilience Platform

Catalyst Grit v2.0.0 joins the full recovery workflow without collapsing its source records. The connected workflow has twelve traceable stages: setback, context, condition mapping, recovery assessment, action plan, checkpoint, reassessment, learning loop, system adaptation, decision handoff, monitoring review, and governed publication.

Each step retains its source entity, identifier, content hash, review requirement, reviewer, and append-only transition history. A workflow may remain active or require review; missing artifacts are never silently invented.

The artifact graph connects records, datasets, analyses, evidence, decisions, monitoring snapshots, publications, and external artifacts across Sustainable Catalyst products. Connections preserve both endpoint hashes and expose stale or conflicting state rather than overwriting provenance.

## Safety boundary

The platform supports structured reflection, recovery planning, and human-systems learning. It prohibits diagnosis, character or personality scoring, employee ranking, hidden performance evaluation, and automated employment or eligibility decisions. Human review is mandatory before institutional interpretation, publication, or cross-product action.
