# Review Checklist

Before using a Catalyst Grit output, review:

- Is the challenge described concretely?
- Are pressure and constraints named?
- Is the severity reasonable?
- Are available supports realistic?
- Are recovery actions specific enough to act on?
- Is the time horizon plausible?
- Does the decision note avoid blame, diagnosis, or overclaiming?
- Are privacy-sensitive details removed before sharing?
