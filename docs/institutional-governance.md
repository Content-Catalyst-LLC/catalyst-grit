# Institutional Governance

Version 2.0.0 adds versioned retention, export/redaction, access-review, methodology-governance, and schema-deprecation policies. Policies may be global or project-specific and active policy revisions retire the prior active revision without deleting history.

Access reviews are append-only and cover members, API clients, publications, or projects. Methodology profiles are registered by name, version, content hash, approval state, approver, and effective date. Schema deprecations record replacement versions, sunset dates, and migration guidance.

Administrative diagnostics report database integrity, migration state, active policy and API-client counts, publication counts, overdue access reviews, and core privacy/audit guarantees. Diagnostics do not expose tokens or private record content.
